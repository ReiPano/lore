# BM25

BM25 is a lexical ranking function that scores documents by term frequency and
inverse document frequency. It excels at queries with identifiers, proper
nouns, and rare terms — the kind of needles that dense vectors tend to
smooth over. SQLite FTS5 ships a `bm25()` function whose scores are non-positive
(lower is better); negating them gives a conventional "higher is better"
ranking that slots cleanly into reciprocal rank fusion.
